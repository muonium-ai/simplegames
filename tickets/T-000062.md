- 2026-05-21: Added ESC-to-quit handling to all four card-game entry points per T-000058 spec: blackjack/blackjack.py, klondike/klondike.py (added sys import), solitaire/Solitaire.py, and solitaire-klondike/src/gamemanager.py (added sys import).
